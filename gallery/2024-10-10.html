When I started using a cup, it tremendously alleviated my gender dysphoria and sensory issues, 'cuz I don't feel the cup at all and thus am not reminded of the period. But also my cup is painted in silly pansexual colours, which really brightens my day every time I see it. It just makes it more about fun than distress! So why can't we have some nice things?
