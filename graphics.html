Flags

I design queer flags for fun sometimes. These are not replacements, just alternatives that I personally like. Feel free to use for whatever you want.


Autigender. Red represents #RedInstead. Golden represents gold as a chemical element that has symbol Au. Rainbow represents a spectrum.

Gendervague. Gray represents fogginess.

Neuroqueer. For both intersection of queerness and neurodivergence, and the practive of neuroqueering as described by Nick Walker.

Intersex and disability inclusive progress Pride flag. I redesigned the flag made by u/it_couldbe_worse_. I removed asymmetry and made it adhere to the logic of the disability Pride flag that is meant to be easy on the eyes.
